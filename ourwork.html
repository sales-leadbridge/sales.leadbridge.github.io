How We Work — Process Overview

We combine automated signals with human validation to deliver targeted leads that convert. Below is our repeatable process designed for scale and quality.

Discovery & ICP

We define your ideal customer and campaign targets — geography, role, company size, and intent signals.

Multi-Source Collection

Harvesting from public records, professional platforms, listings and marketing channels to build candidate lists.

Human Validation

Phone & email checks plus role verification to remove bounce risks and find decision-makers.

Enrichment & Tagging

Each lead is enriched with firmographics, tags and segmentation for targeted outreach.

Delivery Formats

CSV, Google Sheets, or CRM-ready lists with campaign columns for fast execution.

Ongoing Support

Weekly refreshes, sample previews, and optimization support to improve conversion.
